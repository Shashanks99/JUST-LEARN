SAFTY TIPS-WHEN YOU ARE RIDING

11th-17th JAN 2023
ROAD SAFETY WEEK

Use a helmet

Use good quality helmets-preferably light coloured with reflector strips

Use daytime running lights

Use daytime running lights or DRL in daytime to improve visibility and use low beam at night to avoid blinding oncoming traffics

Use side view mirrors

Use side view mirror to remain alert of traffic approaching from the rear

Do not folleo a vehicle too close

Make sure you do not follow a vehicle too close in order to avoid collision,Use the second rule

Do not drink and ride

Keep out of a car or vehicles blind spot
